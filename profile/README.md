LinkiSwap is an intent-powered financial execution network designed to simplify how people interact with digital assets across blockchain ecosystems.

Today's digital asset landscape is fragmented. Users must navigate multiple wallets, chains, bridges, exchanges, and payment providers just to complete simple financial actions.

LinkiSwap removes this complexity by allowing users to focus on outcomes while the platform handles execution behind the scenes.

Whether you're buying crypto, swapping assets, sending funds, cashing out, or spending digital assets in the real world, LinkiSwap provides a unified experience across multiple blockchain networks.

## Our Mission

We believe users should not need to understand blockchain infrastructure to benefit from digital assets.

Most people don't care about:

* Bridges
* Gas tokens
* Liquidity routes
* Settlement mechanisms
* Cross-chain messaging

They care about outcomes.

Examples:

* Send money to a friend
* Swap one asset for another
* Buy digital assets
* Cash out to a bank account
* Purchase gift cards

LinkiSwap handles the complexity so users can focus on what they want to accomplish.

## The Evolution of LinkiSwap

### V1: Aggregation

LinkiSwap began as a unified platform that integrated multiple providers to simplify digital asset transactions.

Through a single interface, users could access:

* Swaps
* On-ramp services
* Off-ramp services
* Cross-chain transfers
* Gift card services

### V2: Intent-Based Execution

LinkiSwap V2 introduces an intent-driven architecture.

Instead of manually selecting chains, bridges, and routes, users simply express what they want to achieve.

For example:

Instead of:

```text
Select Chain
→ Select Token
→ Select Bridge
→ Confirm Route
→ Execute
```

Users simply state:

```text
Send $100 to Sarah
```

LinkiSwap determines the best way to execute that request.

LinkiSwap V2 will support multiple solvers competing to execute user intents efficiently.

This creates a scalable execution network capable of supporting increasingly complex financial workflows across blockchain ecosystems.
